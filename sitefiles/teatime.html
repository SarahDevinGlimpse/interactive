Ingredients

  • 1 blank html file
  • 1 serving content of interest
  • 1 decent chunk of time
  • 1 hearty helping of patience
  • Aesthetic opinions, to taste

Instructions

  1. Pour yourself 1 cup of boiling water. A clear canvas.

  2. Open up your tea chest and pick 1 teabag; OR mix your own loose leaf tea; OR steep 2 bags at once. The flavor is up to you.

  3. The longer your tea bag steeps, the richer the flavor. Experiment with different steeping times. Everyone has their own.

  4. Add your personal touch. Perhaps you prefer one cube of sugar, or two. A splash of creamer. Skim milk, almond milk, soy milk, coconut milk. Honey and lemon. A sprinkling of cinnamon, perhaps. Mix in the add-ins of your choice.

  5. Not everyone takes their tea the same way. Take some time to figure out what you like before worrying about other people's tea tastes.
